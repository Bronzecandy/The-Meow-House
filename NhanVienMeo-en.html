Pursue Cá-reer With Cats For A Rescue Cause!

Currently, Pawfect Match SGN is the exclusive partner of Meow House in supporting the fostering and adoption procedures. Let's explore the steps to hire" a "cat employee" for your home:

#1: Visit Meow House and meet the adorable rescue cats.

#2: If you find a cat you adore, quickly access The Cá-reer Space website or directly contact our staff to gather information about the cat.

#3: Message Pawfect Match SGN to register your interest in "hiring" the cat you love.

#4: Complete all the required information in the form provided by Pawfect Match SGN.

#5: Be patient and wait for 1-2 weeks for Pawfect Match SGN to review your "hiring" application.

#6: If your application is approved, complete the "hiring" process by paying a small fee to support the rescue efforts of the station, and schedule the date to welcome your new "cat employee" home!
